Designing with Intelligence

IDExAI.26 is a pilot educational program at Özyeğin University’s Department of Industrial Design, exploring how generative AI can be integrated into studio-based design education.

This exhibition presents student projects from Year 1–4 studios, documenting how emerging AI tools are shaping contemporary design workflows.
